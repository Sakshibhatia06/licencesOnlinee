What is Web & E-Commerce Development?

It’s the process of designing and developing websites that are not only visually appealing but also functional, secure, and scalable. For e-commerce, this includes product catalogs, cart systems, secure checkout, order tracking, and backend automation. We work with modern stacks and platforms like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Liquid (Shopify), React, and Next.js to ensure pixel-perfect, high-speed results.

Step-by-Step Web & E-Commerce Development Process

Whether you need a custom-coded business site or a powerful Shopify e-commerce store, our web development process is streamlined, collaborative, and result-driven. Here’s how we take your vision online:

Detailed Process:

  1. Requirement Gathering & Consultation: We begin with a deep dive into your goals, industry, design preferences, and features — whether it's a Shopify store or a custom HTML/CSS/JS build.
  2. Design & Wireframe: Based on your brand identity, we create UI/UX wireframes or mockups that define your site's structure, layout, and user flow across devices.
  3. Development & Integration: Our developers build your site using custom code (HTML, CSS, JS, React, etc.) or develop using Shopify with advanced features, speed optimization, and responsive design.
  4. Testing & Revisions: We rigorously test for performance, responsiveness, SEO, and browser compatibility. Revisions are made based on your feedback.
  5. Launch & Handover: Once approved, we deploy your website to your domain and server. We also provide documentation, admin access, and optional post-launch support.
