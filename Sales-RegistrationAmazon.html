Amazon / Flipkart Selling FAQs

Who can sell on Amazon and Flipkart?

Any individual, firm, or company with a valid GST number and a bank account can register and sell on Amazon or Flipkart. You must comply with each platform’s seller policies.

Is GST mandatory for registration?

Yes, GST is required for most product categories. Exceptions may apply for specific book or handicraft items, but a GSTIN is usually essential to register as a seller.

What documents are needed to become a seller?

You need a GST certificate, PAN card, bank account details, business address proof, and signature authorization. Brand authorization is needed for branded products.

Can I sell branded products?

Yes, but brand approval is required. You’ll need brand authorization letters or trademark certificates for listing branded items on Amazon or Flipkart.

How long does the seller registration process take?

If all documents are in order, registration and KYC approval typically take 1–3 working days. Listing setup and go-live may take a few more days.

Do I need a website to start selling?

No, you don’t need a website. Amazon and Flipkart act as your selling platform. However, having your own website is useful for branding and building customer trust.

How do I list products on these platforms?

Products can be listed manually or in bulk using Excel sheets. We assist in creating optimized listings with proper images, pricing, tax codes, and category mapping.

Can you help me manage my seller account?

Yes. We offer account management services including catalog uploads, stock updates, ad campaigns, order support, returns handling, and health score monitoring.

What are the charges for registration and listing?

Our service fees are affordable and depend on the scope — such as registration, brand approval, product listing, or full account setup. Platform commissions vary by category.

Will I receive support after the account is live?

Absolutely. We provide ongoing support for account troubleshooting, catalog edits, policy compliance, seasonal sale setup, and performance optimization.
