Flipkart Seller FAQs

Who can sell on Flipkart?

Any individual, firm, or business with a valid GST number, PAN card, and a bank account can register as a Flipkart seller. Compliance with Flipkart's seller policies is mandatory.

Is GST required to register on Flipkart?

Yes, GST is mandatory for most categories. However, some exceptions exist for books or select handcrafted items. For all other listings, a valid GSTIN is required.

What documents are needed to become a Flipkart seller?

You need a GST certificate, PAN card, active bank account, business address proof, and signature authorization. For branded products, trademark or brand authorization may be required.

Can I sell branded items on Flipkart?

Yes, but you’ll need to get brand approval from Flipkart. This involves submitting brand authorization letters or trademark certificates to unlock branded product listings.

How long does Flipkart seller registration take?

If your documents are correct, seller registration and KYC verification usually take 1–3 working days. Product listing and store activation may take additional time based on catalog size.

Do I need a website to sell on Flipkart?

No, a website is not required. Flipkart provides a complete platform for product listing, order management, payments, and logistics — no separate website needed.

How do I list my products on Flipkart?

You can list products manually or in bulk via Excel sheet uploads. We assist in creating SEO-optimized listings with high-quality images, accurate pricing, tax details, and keyword targeting.

Can you manage my Flipkart seller account?

Yes, we offer complete account management — including product uploads, pricing updates, inventory sync, ad campaigns, order assistance, returns processing, and policy compliance.

What are your charges for registration and listing?

Our pricing is flexible based on the services you choose — registration, catalog creation, brand approval, or full account management. Flipkart's own platform fees vary by product category.

Will I get support after my Flipkart store goes live?

Absolutely! We provide post-launch support including performance monitoring, product edits, seasonal campaign setup, account health improvement, and issue resolution.
